About Group Chairman

G.M Lingaraju is one of the key industrialists from Karnataka, India. Who had been officially working with the GM Group of companies for the past two decades. His prolific and 'out-of-the-box' perspective and insight in the innovation and devise have a great significance in the rise of the GM Group of Companies.

 

G.M. Lingaraju's rich portfolio has repeatedly demonstrated the aspirations for a high level performance and have also created an effective socio-economic framework for the GM Group. His leadership initiatives were successfully imbibed from Late Shri G. Mallikarjunappa, the founder of the G.M. Group of companies, who was a great visionary and was also a Honourable Member of Parliament, from Davangere, Karnataka.

 

 G.M Lingaraju's high involvement in the family business has manifested the business system on a well demonstrated foundation with tangible results in the service industry. Combined with an avid and deep knowledge, he is managing a high circularized team of diversified businesses owned by the GM Group and Companies.

 

G.M Lingaraju's core competency is in tailoring new prospects, by prioritizing customer and employee satisfaction, as its core fundamental responsibilities.

G.M. Lingaraju
